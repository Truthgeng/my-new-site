PitchProtocol Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

PitchProtocol values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal info you provide: name, email, profile info, and content you input into PitchProtocol (e.g., pitches, CV info).
  • Usage info: browser type, device, IP address, and interactions with the tool.
  • Analytics data: anonymous data to help improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain PitchProtocol services.
  • To send updates, tips, or newsletters related to the 30-day challenge or your account.
  • To analyze trends, improve features, and understand user behavior.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We may share anonymized or aggregated data for analytics.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Security

We take your data seriously and use reasonable measures to protect it, including encryption and secure servers. We also rely on trusted third-party services for hosting, email, and analytics.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to the data we hold about you.
  • You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • You can unsubscribe from emails or newsletters anytime.

6. Cookies and Tracking

PitchProtocol may use cookies or analytics tools to improve your experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted here with the effective date.
